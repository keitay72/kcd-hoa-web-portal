# Customer Verification Flow

Target customer signup uses address match plus email verification:

1. Customer enters service address and email on the tenant-branded portal.
2. The system checks whether the service address exists for that tenant.
3. If eligible, the system creates a pending registration and sends a verification email.
4. Customer clicks the email link.
5. Customer completes password, name, phone, and profile setup.
6. The account becomes active and linked to the verified service location.

Activation codes are optional compatibility or strict-mode verification, not the default customer onboarding path.
